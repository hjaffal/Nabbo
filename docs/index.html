How it works

Three steps. From chaos to clarity in seconds.

📨

1. Capture

Share from any app, forward an email, or just speak. Nabbo accepts school emails, WhatsApp messages, screenshots, voice notes — anything.

🧠

2. Understand

AI extracts the actions: who, what, when, where, what to bring, what to pay. Not a summary — real operational meaning.

✅

3. Review & Go

Quick-approve what Nabbo found, or tap to edit. Approved items become your family's clear plan — with reminders and checklists.
