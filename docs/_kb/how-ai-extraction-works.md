title: How AI Extraction Works
updated: July 2025
---

Nabbo uses advanced AI to turn unstructured messages into clear, actionable items for your family. Here's how it works.

## What Nabbo Extracts

When you share a message, email, or voice note, Nabbo's AI identifies:

- **Events** — activities with a date, time, and often a location (school trips, football practice, birthday parties)
- **Tasks** — things that need doing (sign a form, pack a lunch, buy supplies)
- **Deadlines** — dates by which something must happen (payment due, RSVP by, registration closes)

For each item, Nabbo extracts:

- **Who** — which family member is involved
- **What** — the activity or action
- **When** — date and time (or deadline)
- **Where** — location if mentioned
- **Notes** — extra details like what to bring, costs, links, or contact info

## Confidence Levels

Nabbo assigns a confidence level to each extraction:

- **High confidence** — the information was clearly stated (explicit date, named person)
- **Medium confidence** — the AI inferred details from context (e.g. "next Friday" resolved to a specific date)
- **Low confidence** — the AI is less certain and flags the item for careful review

Items with high confidence may be auto-approved (if you've enabled that in Settings). Lower confidence items always appear in your review queue.

## How to Review Items

After extraction, items appear in your **Review** tab:

1. **Approve** — tap to confirm the item as-is and add it to your plan
2. **Edit** — tap to adjust any detail (date, time, person, notes) before approving
3. **Dismiss** — swipe to remove items that aren't relevant

## Change Detection

If you share a message that updates an existing item (e.g. "Football moved to 5pm" when you already have football at 4pm), Nabbo detects this and shows you what changed rather than creating a duplicate.

## Multimodal Input

Nabbo doesn't just read text. It can also process:

- **Images** — screenshots of messages, photos of flyers or notices
- **PDFs** — permission slips, school letters, booking confirmations
- **Voice** — natural speech converted to structured items

## Tips for Best Results

- Forward the full email rather than copying fragments
- Include context when sharing screenshots (Nabbo reads surrounding text too)
- Speak in natural sentences when using voice input
- The more you use Nabbo, the better it learns your household's patterns
