---
title: How Email Forwarding Works
updated: July 2025
---

Nabbo gives every household a unique email address so you can forward messages directly from your inbox.

## Find Your Nabbo Email

1. Open Nabbo and go to **Settings**
2. Under your household name, you'll see your unique email alias (e.g. `smithfamily@nabboapp.com`)
3. Tap to copy it to your clipboard

## Forward a Message

From your email client (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.):

1. Open the email you want Nabbo to process
2. Tap **Forward**
3. Paste your Nabbo email address as the recipient
4. Send it

Nabbo will receive the email within seconds, extract the key details, and present them in your review queue.

## What Works Best

Email forwarding is ideal for:

- **School newsletters** with multiple dates, events, and deadlines
- **Booking confirmations** (activities, classes, appointments)
- **Payment reminders** with due dates and amounts
- **Event invitations** with times, locations, and requirements

## Tips

- You can forward the same email multiple times — Nabbo's change detection will recognise duplicates
- HTML emails work great — Nabbo reads both the text and structured content
- Attachments like PDFs and images are processed too (e.g. permission slips, flyers)
- Set up auto-forwarding rules in your email client for regular senders like your child's school

## Troubleshooting

If a forwarded email doesn't appear in Nabbo:

- Check your Nabbo email alias is correct (no typos)
- Give it 30 seconds to process
- Check your email client's "Sent" folder to confirm it sent successfully
- If the issue persists, contact [support@nabboapp.com](mailto:support@nabboapp.com)
