Welcome to Nabbo! This guide will walk you through setting up your household and capturing your first item.

## Create Your Household

When you first open Nabbo, you'll be asked to create your household. This involves:

1. **Name your household** — give it a name (e.g. "The Smiths" or "Home")
2. **Set your timezone** — so deadlines and reminders arrive at the right time
3. **Set your location** — helps with weather and local context

## Add Family Members

Navigate to Settings → Family Members to add the people in your household:

- Tap **Add Member** and enter their name
- Assign a role (parent, child, other)
- Each member gets an auto-assigned colour (you can change it later)
- Optionally add a photo

Nabbo uses family members to assign items to the right person when extracting from messages.

## Capture Your First Item

There are three ways to get information into Nabbo:

### Share from another app
Open any message, email, or screenshot in another app, tap **Share**, and select Nabbo. The AI will extract the key actions automatically.

### Forward an email
In Settings, you'll find your household's unique Nabbo email address. Forward any email to it — school newsletters, booking confirmations, payment reminders — and Nabbo will read and extract the details.

### Speak it
Tap the microphone button and speak naturally: "Ella has swimming on Tuesday at 4pm, bring goggles and towel." Nabbo will create the structured item for you.

## Review & Approve

After capturing, Nabbo's AI extracts the important details and presents them for your review. Tap **Approve** to confirm, or **Edit** to adjust anything before it joins your family plan.
