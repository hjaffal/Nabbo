# Features

Nabbo turns your family's messy messages into clear actions — automatically.

---

## 📨 Capture from Anywhere

Share content into Nabbo from any app on your phone. No copying, no re-typing.

- **Share from WhatsApp, Messages, email, browsers** — tap share, pick Nabbo
- **Forward emails** — your household gets a unique Nabbo email address
- **Voice capture** — speak naturally, Nabbo transcribes and extracts
- **Photo capture** — snap a school notice, Nabbo reads it
- **Text input** — quick notes typed directly in the app

---

## 🧠 AI Extraction

Nabbo doesn't just store your messages — it understands them.

- **Events** — dates, times, locations, who's affected
- **Deadlines** — payments due, forms to submit, registration windows
- **Tasks** — things to pack, actions to take, people to contact
- **Change detection** — time moved? Cancelled? Nabbo catches it
- **Multi-item extraction** — one school email can produce 5+ actionable items
- **Recurring events** — "every Tuesday" becomes a proper recurring item

---

## ✅ Review & Approve

Nothing enters your plan without your say-so. Quick, not tedious.

- **Review cards** — see what Nabbo found at a glance
- **One-tap approve** — confident extractions approved instantly
- **Edit before approving** — fix a date or child assignment in seconds
- **Auto-approval** — high-confidence items skip review entirely (configurable)
- **Uncertainty flags** — Nabbo shows you what it's not sure about

---

## ☀️ Morning Brief

Open Nabbo and see your day in one card.

- Today's events, deadlines, and tasks
- Items still needing review
- Anything with no owner assigned
- Changes since yesterday

---

## 👶 Per-Child Week View

Tap a child's name to see their full week.

- Monday through Sunday layout
- Events, tasks, and deadlines per day
- Recurring items expanded automatically
- Time + location + category at a glance

---

## 📋 Activity Feed

See what's been happening in your household.

- Approvals, completions, captures, cancellations
- Real-time updates
- Tap any event to see the related item
- Unread badge shows new activity

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Members

Nabbo knows your family and assigns items automatically.

- Add children with color-coded avatars
- AI matches "football" → Adam, "swimming" → Yara
- Ambiguity picker when it's not sure which child
- Adults as owners for pickups, payments, forms

---

## 🔄 Change Detection

When plans change, Nabbo catches it before you miss it.

- Time or date changed
- Location changed
- Event cancelled
- New requirement added
- Side-by-side comparison: old → new

---

## 🔔 Smart Notifications

Helpful nudges, not noise.

- Items ready for review
- Deadlines approaching
- Changes detected
- Events starting soon
- Configurable quiet hours

---

## 🏠 Household Intelligence

Nabbo learns your family's patterns over time.

- Links children to activities, schools, contacts
- Resolves "which child?" without asking
- Gets smarter the more you use it
- Never guesses — flags uncertainty clearly
